“Take Us to Your Chief”

An interactive learning resource exploring Drew Hayden Taylor’s short story through literary devices, themes, and real-world connections.

Story Summary

Three Ojibway men spend their days drinking beer and hanging out on the beach — until one day a spaceship suddenly lands right in front of them. Out of the ship step the Kaaw Wiyaa, an alien species hoping to make diplomatic negotiations with Earth’s leader.

The men take the aliens to their community’s chief, and a discussion is held about sending an ambassador to the Kaaw Wiyaa planet to represent Earth. The chief selects the three men as ambassadors, and so they depart Earth with the aliens, heading for the Kaaw Wiyaa’s home world.
